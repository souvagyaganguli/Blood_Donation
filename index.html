ABOUT US

We are Team of "A Thousand Years".

The purpose of human life is unknown, what we are, why we are, all these are philosophical questions, one whose answer we may know or we may not but the one thing we know for sure is "We exist together."
Through this website we want to make a small contribution to the human society, the one which we live in. This website is a Blood Donation Website which intends to help anyone in need of blood and intends to their life.
Next to creating a life the finest thing a man can do is save one.

WHY SHOULD YOU DONATE BLOOD?

Our nation requires 4 Crore Units of Blood while only 40 lakh units are available, Every two seconds someone needs Blood There is no substitute for Human Blood. It cannot be manufactured
Blood donation is an extremely noble deed, yet there is a scarcity of regular donors across India. We focus on creating & expanding a virtual army of blood donating volunteers who could be searched and contacted by family / care givers of a patient in times of need

Be a Donor

DONATION PROCESS

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

REGISTRATION

You need to complete a very simple registration form. Which contains all required contact information to enter in the donation process.

SCREENING

A drop of blood from your finger will take for simple test to ensure that your blood iron levels are proper enough for donation process.

DONATION

After ensuring and passed screening test successfully you will be directed to a donor bed for donation. It will take only 6-10 minutes.

REFRESHMENT

You can also stay in sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave our center. You will receive awesome drink from us in donation zone.
